Collection

Gamma_love is a collection of 200 images. All images are 1/1, as they were derived from generative input. Each image has one of the phrases, written as 36 blocks of arbitrary designed 'font'. The phrases are: gammagotlove, gammaloveyou, lovegotgamma, lovegotyou, lovethisgamma, loveyougamma, thisgammalove, thisgammayou, yougotthis, and yougotlove. There are three images with completely unique traits. Traits are counted based on the words that are in the images and on the rarity of their appearance as a whole.

Method

Images of words are made in 7x7 px matrix, then generated to randomly display 6 x 6 blocks (matrices)

About m_fused
