Collection

Grlz is a collection of 100 images which tries to answer the question 'What is beauty?'. Images of the female persons from all over the world radiate and invite you to take a closer look. sometimes they look straight at you, and more often the gaze is turned to the side. Bold, strong, sometimes shy, with a twinkle in their eye, beautiful, each of them.

Beauty is a concept that is often difficult to define, as it is deeply rooted in individual and cultural perspectives. For many, beauty is linked to physical qualities such as facial features, body type and skin tone. In the art world, beauty goes beyond physical aesthetics and is often presented in abstract and unique ways. AI-generated images can provide a unique perspective on beauty, which can help to challenge existing notions of beauty and spark new conversations around this topic.

Method

Images derived from hybrid process. First sketches were hand-drawn then custom GAN model was fine-tuned on that data. Later, more and more graphical data was added. At the end, model was tuned to make fine-grained pixelation, to avoid that artificial metal-glare which many AI images of persons have. They are real or comic-book character, they are next door neighbor or Arabian princess, it doesn't matter. They are just beautiful.

About artists

M:Fused is art group working primarily with digital tools. We enjoy researching and experimenting with new techniques, filters, softwares and all stuff digital. Recently we started developing our own ML models and fine-tuning them. We are based in Zagreb, Munich and Venice.
