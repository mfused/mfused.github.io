Collection

Gamma_got_this is a collection of 20 images. All images are 1/1, as they were derived from generative input. Each image has the 'gamma got this' phrase, written as 36 blocks of arbitrary designed 'font'.

Method

Images are made in 7x7 px matrix, then generated to randomly display 6 x 6 blocks (matrices)

About m_fused

M:Fused is art group working primarily with digital tools. We enjoy researching and experimenting with new techniques, filters, softwares and all stuff digital. Recently we started developing our own ML models and fine-tuning them. We are based in Zagreb, Munich and Venice.
